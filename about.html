Meet our CEO Ologe Abigail

I am Ologe Abigail, an indigene of Delta state. My love for food made me come up with Abby's kitchen,I saw many food lovers like me who didn't have an opportunity to eat the food they enjoyed at good quality and at an affordable price. This made me come up with my brand as I want food lovers to enjoy good food at an affordable price in a beautiful environment. My dear food lovers, come to Abby's kitchen and you'll love it.
